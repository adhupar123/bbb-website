Dataset

The analysis uses a curated dataset of molecules with experimental LogBB values. Molecular descriptors were computed with RDKit (MolLogP, MolWt, TPSA, formal charge, aromatic rings, heavy atom count).

Modeling summary

From the notebook: a multiple linear regression was fitted to predict logBB. Example fitted coefficients (from the notebook):

  • Intercept: 0.2422
  • MolLogP: 0.0052
  • MolWt: -0.0012
  • TPSA: -0.0127
  • Formal charge (FC): 0.0553
  • Aromatic rings: -0.1340
  • Heavy atoms: 0.0464

Note: coefficients here are illustrative — see the notebook for training details, splits, and diagnostics.

How to interpret

Positive coefficients suggest a descriptor increases logBB (higher permeability); negative coefficients suggest a reduction. For example, aromatic rings had a negative coefficient in the reported fit.
